Patients and methods
Data on all hip fracture patients admitted to a large dis-
trict hospital between January 1989 to August 2021 were 
prospectively collected on a hip fracture database using 
a standardised proforma. Data collected included: age, 
gender, place of residence prior to admission, American 
Society of Anaesthesiologist (ASA) score, mini mental test

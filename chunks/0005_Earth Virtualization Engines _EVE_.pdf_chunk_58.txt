2 Frequently asked questions
(1) Are scientists really “surprised” by the
changes?

ways. To be clear, climate science has long established why Earth is
warming and has developed models that have explained its broad
trends and contours. Scientists understand, in general terms, how
some forms of extremes might change with warming. What is

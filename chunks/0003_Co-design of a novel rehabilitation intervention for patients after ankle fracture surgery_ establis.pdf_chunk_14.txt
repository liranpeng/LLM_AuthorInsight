Study design
This study was incorporated within the weight-bearing in ankle fractures
(WAX) trial, published separately [15]. WAX was a multicentre,
randomised controlled trial conducted in the UK that aimed to study early
weight-bearing following operatively managed ankle fractures compared
to traditional delayed weight-bearing regimens. A protocol for the WAX
trial was registered on the 2nd of December 2019 (ISRCTN12883981)

(10) How was EVE's budget estimated?
The EUR 300 million per center per year price tag was estimated
based on the current budget of international organizations whose
profiles overlap with parts of EVE's remit, and it anticipates a
roughly equal split between funding for staff, running costs (mostly
power), and investments (hardware procurements).

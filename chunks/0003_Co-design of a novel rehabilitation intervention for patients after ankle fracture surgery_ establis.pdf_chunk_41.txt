The survey asked respondents if they would consider discharging
patients from clinic sooner than usual if the patient had access to
credible recovery information and could book back into clinic quickly if
they had problems. There was a consensus that HCPs would consider
discharging patients with these provisions at six weeks, but only 43% of
HCPs would consider this at two weeks post-operatively. Almost three-
quarters of HCPs would like to review their aggregated patient outcomes

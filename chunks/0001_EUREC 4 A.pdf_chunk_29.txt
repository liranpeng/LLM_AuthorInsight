Abstract. The science guiding the EUREC4A campaign and its measurements is presented. EUREC4A com-
prised roughly 5 weeks of measurements in the downstream winter trades of the North Atlantic – eastward and
southeastward of Barbados. Through its ability to characterize processes operating across a wide range of scales,
EUREC4A marked a turning point in our ability to observationally study factors inﬂuencing clouds in the trades,
